University Town College Programme

I have spent my two years in NUS staying and studying in Tembusu College as part of the UTC Programme. Tembusu College is also home to many of the Interest Groups (IG). I am specifically committed to two IGs:

  • Tembusu Table Tennis (TTT) as the Captain

    • GreenT is an environmental interest group that aims to induce environmental friendly habits to our fellow Tembusians. Furthermore, we also hold major events like eco-sale and environmental dialogue.
